It contains information on bank customers who either left the bank or continue to be a customer. 
The dataset includes the following attributes:

1. Credit Score: A numerical value representing the customer's credit score

2. Geography: The country where the customer resides (France, Spain or Germany)

3. Gender: The customer's gender (Male or Female)

4. Age: The customer's age.

5. Tenure: The number of years the customer has been with the bank

6. Balance: The customer's account balance

7. NumOfProducts: The number of bank products the customer uses (e.g., savings account, credit card)

8. HasCrCard: Whether the customer has a credit card (1 = yes, 0 = no)

9. IsActiveMember: Whether the customer is an active member (1 = yes, 0 = no)

10. EstimatedSalary: The estimated salary of the customer

11. Exited: Whether the customer has churned (1 = yes, 0 = no)
